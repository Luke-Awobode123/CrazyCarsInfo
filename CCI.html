LAMBORGHINI VENENO

Information about the Lamborghini Veneno:

Inspiration

The Lamborghini Veneno was inspired
by The Lamborghini Aventador.

Speed:

The Lamborghini Veneno travels at a top speed of 356 km/h
(221 mph) and has a 0–97 km/h (0–60 mph) acceleration time of
2.9 seconds.

Price

The Lamborghini Veneno cost a whopping $4.5 million, making
it one of the most expensive cars in history.

Designers

The Lamborghini Veneno was designed by Filippo Perini
and Michele Tinazzo

Production Year

The Lamborghini Veneno was produced in the years 2013-14 with only 13 in existence (4 coupés, 9 roadsters)

FERRARI LaFERRARI APERTA

Information about the Ferrari LaFerrari Aperta:

Inspiration

The Ferrari LaFerrari Aperta is the open-top version of
The Ferrari LaFerrari

Speed:

The Ferrari LaFerrari Aperta travels at a top speed of 346 km/h
(215 mph) and has a 0–100 km/h (0–62 mph) acceleration time of
2.6 seconds.

Price

The Ferrari LaFerrari Aperta cost a whopping $5.4 million, making
it one of the most expensive cars in history.

Designers

The Ferrari LaFerrari Aperta was designed by Flavio Manzoni.

Production Year

The Ferrari LaFerrari Aperta was produced in the years 2016-18 with only 210 in existence

BUGATTI CENTODIECI

Information about the Bugatti Centodieci:

Inspiration

The Bugatti Centodieci is inspired by the legendary EB110 Supersport

Speed:

The Bugatti Centodieci travels at a top speed of 380 km/h
(240 mph) and has a 0–100 km/h (0–62 mph) acceleration time of
2.4 seconds.

Price

The Bugatti Centodieci cost a whopping $9 million, making
it the 4th expensive car in history.

Designers

The Bugatti Centodieci was designed by Achim Anscheidt and Nils Sajonz.

Production Year

The Bugatti Centodieci was produced in the year 2022-06 to 2022-12 with only 10 in existence
